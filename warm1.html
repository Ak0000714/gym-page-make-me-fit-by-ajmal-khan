WARM UPS

Jumping Jacks (2 minutes)

  1. Start with your feet together and arms at your sides.
  2. - Jump while spreading your legs and raising your arms overhead.
  3. - Return to the starting position by jumping again.
  4. - This exercise raises your heart rate and warms up your entire body.

Arm Circles (1 minute)

  1. Extend your arms out to the sides

  2. - Make small circles with your arms, gradually increasing the circle size.
  3. - After 30 seconds, reverse the direction of the circles.

  4. This warms up your shoulder joints and upper body.

Leg Swings (1 minute each leg)

  1. Hold onto a stable surface for balance.
  2. - Swing one leg forward and backward in a controlled manner
    3. .
  3. - After 30 seconds, switch to the other leg.
  4. This stretches and warms up your hip and leg muscles.

Bodyweight Squats (1 minute):

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart
  2. - Lower your body by bending your knees, as if you're sitting in a chair.
  3. - Keep your back straight and chest up.
  4. - Rise back up to the starting position.
  5. - This prepares your legs and lower back for squats or leg exercises.
