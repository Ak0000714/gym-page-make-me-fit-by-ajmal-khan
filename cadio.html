CADIO WORKOUT

    Cardio exercises (choose any of these or mix them up):

  • 1. Jumping Jacks: Do 3 sets of 30 seconds each with a 10-second rest between sets.
  • 2. High Knees: Run in place, bringing your knees as high as possible for 1 minute, followed by a 20-second rest.
  • 3. Burpees: Perform 3 sets of 10 repetitions with a 15-second rest between sets.
  • 4. Jump Rope: Jump rope continuously for 2-3 minutes.
  • 5. Mountain Climbers: Do 3 sets of 20 seconds with a 10-second rest between sets
