DEIT PLAN FOR WEIGHT LOSS

    Breakfast (within 1-2 hours of waking up)

  • Option 1:Whole-grain cereal with low-fat milk and berries.
  • Option 2: Oatmeal topped with sliced fruits and a sprinkle of nuts.
  • option 3:Greek yogurt with honey and a handful of almonds.

    Mid-morning snack (around 10 am):

  • Option 1: Apple slices with peanut butter.
  • Option 2:Carrot sticks with hummus.
  • Option 3: Mixed berries and a small portion of cottage cheese.

    Lunch (around 12:30 - 1 pm):

  • Option 1: Grilled chicken breast with a side of quinoa and steamed vegetables.
  • Option 2:Spinach salad with chickpeas, tomatoes, cucumbers, and a light vinaigrette dressing.
  • option 3:Whole-grain wrap with lean turkey, avocado, lettuce, and tomato.
>

    Afternoon snack (around 3 pm):

  • Option 1:Handful of mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, and pistachios).
  • Option 2: Sliced cucumber with a sprinkle of lemon juice and chili powder
  • Option 3: Rice cakes with cottage cheese and cherry tomatoes.

    Dinner (around 6:30 - 7 pm):

  • Option 1: Baked salmon with quinoa and roasted vegetables.
  • Option 2: Stir-fried tofu with broccoli, bell peppers, and brown rice.
  • option 3: Lentil soup with a side of whole-grain bread.
